My Story

What is Hotdog Technology and how did it get started?

Over 35 years ago my uncle Art started selling Hot Dogs on a street corner (in Los Angeles) right outside of the old Farmers Market on San Pedro Street. My uncle Art lived by a philosophy of hard work and dedication. He also believed in selling only the best Hot Dog at an honest price.

When I was finally old enough to work, my dad (who was a successful business owner at the time) made the decision to have me work with my uncle during the summers of 1986 through 1989. Selling Hot Dogs was hard work but the best part of working with my uncle Art was that I would inevitably get to have a couple of the Best Hot Dogs west of the Rockies.

A few years after my initial exposure to the culinary art of making mouthwatering Hot Dogs I went to work with my dad at his paint manufacturing plant (Engineered Coating Technology). Where I learned Systems Admin, so that I could setup my stepmom’s workstation at home, so that I could (to a small extent) enjoy my stepmom’s absence while I worked.

After years of reminiscing about the “good old days”, during an afternoon of daydreaming I had a vision, a vision of this. And that’s how Hotdog Technology was born. Hotdog Technology is a repository of my personal and professional experiences, the mistakes I’ve made, how to avoid making them, how I took control of my finances and how do I now help my friends and family make money though the use of wise investment strategies.
